Baked Potato Recipe

For Affordable Meal Prepping

This recipe for baked potatoes can help keep four people from feeling too hungry to sleep using easy to find ingredients at a reasonable cost, even in the wintertime. It's an easy 1st recipe for a new apartment

Ingredients:

  • 1 Bag of Russet Potatoes
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
    • sharp cheddar
    • or extra sharp
  • Sour Cream
  • Butter
    • salted
    • or unsalted
  • Salt
    • iodized salt
    • or kosher salt
  • H2O

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. Rinse Russet Potatoes
  3. Dissolve salt into warm water in a shallow dish
  4. Stab each potato with a sturdy fork 6 times
    (to allow steam to escape during baking)
  5. Roll/soak potatoes in salty water
  6. Bake directly on oven rack for 1 hour
    testing for fork tenderness before removal
  7. Slice potatoes once down the center
    (to allow more steam to escape and make room for toppings)

Suggestions for Toppings:

  • Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Butter
